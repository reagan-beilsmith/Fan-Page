Why I like chocolate- covered foods:

  • Texture: depends, but usually crunchy then soft.
  • Flavor: My personal favorites are chocolate-covered strawberries, cheesecake, and marshmallows.
  • Unique Aspect: you can coat pretty much any food in chocolate, although I wouldn't always recommend it.
  • Overall: Not bad. Sometimes difficult to eat when it crumbles, but overall fun and high variety. 8/10.
